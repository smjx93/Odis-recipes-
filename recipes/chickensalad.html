Chicken Salad with Apples, Grapes, and Walnuts!

Description

I had chicken breasts left over from a BBQ I had and decided to make a chicken salad. I couldn't find a recipe that I liked so I played around and came up with this. It is delicious!

Ingredients

  • 4 cooked chicken breasts, shredded
  • 2 Granny Smith apples, cut into small chunks
  • 2 cups chopped walnuts, or to taste
  • ½ red onion, chopped
  • 3 stalks celery, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • ½ cup vanilla yogurt
  • 5 tablespoons creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip®)
  • 5 tablespoons mayonnaise 25 seedless red grapes, halved

Directions

  • Step 1 Toss shredded chicken, apple chunks, walnuts, red onion, celery, and lemon juice together in a large bowl.
  • Step 2 Whisk vanilla yogurt, salad dressing, and mayonnaise together in a separate bowl; pour over the chicken mixture and stir to coat. Gently fold the grapes into the salad.
